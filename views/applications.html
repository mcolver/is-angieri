Fleeting Beauty

Fleeking Beauty allows users to unleash their aesthetic judgments upon the internet presence of any place on earth. Users vote on photos of an unnamed place, judging whether they deem them to be "hot" or "not". We store users' vote data and display it visually using graphs.
